What is Food Luck Limited?

　HIGH QUALITY, DELECTABLE, CONFIDENCE

　Food Luck Limited has tight connections with local farms, marine companies and pastures in Japan, ensuring fresh and delicious food from Japan could be imported to Hong Kong safely and directly

　OUR VISION

　We aim to build a connection between Japan and Hong Kong, introducing different catering cultures from Japan to Hong Kong, allowing Hong Kong people could try the original taste of Japan from their home

Services that Food Luck Limited provides

  1. (1)Trading Japanese food products to Hong Kong
  2. (2)Consulting on food imports and exports
  3. (3)E-commerce channel for sales
